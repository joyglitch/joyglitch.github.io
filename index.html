About Me

Hello there! My name is Joyce Li, and I am in my last year of being an Engineering Systems & Computing undergraduate student at the University of Guelph (UG). I am expecting to graduate soon in May 2021.

I worked for SUEZ as a Lab Research Assistant to create material sales specification documents and to conduct lab experiments on the quality of product water treatment. Then, I worked for Moen Canada as an Engineering Analyst to perform data mining, develop reports, update floorplans, and other engineering tasks. Currently, I work as a Research Assistant for an Associate Professor at UG to develop an environmental agent-based model that can simulate the spread of COVID-19 in a city.

Some of my hobbies and interests include programming, machine learning, big data analytics, hands-on engineering projects, and reading. I am recently working on a few personal projects.
